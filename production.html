Passionate craftsmanship

We love creating the best toys for you. We spend a lot of our time inside our shop.
We set up the wood by making planks that will be perfect for all our designs, they vary from 2 to 6 cms. We carefully cut the wood into smaller pieces by using an industrial saw for the biggest pieces and a laser saw for the small ones. These last pieces require attention to small details, so a lot of the time are finished by hand with smaller tools.
