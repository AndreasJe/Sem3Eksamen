Unboxing experience

Once all the little pieces are ready, it’s time for departure.

Every little piece is carefully scrutinised and packed into a recycled cardboard box together with our beautifully designed instructions.

We make very small toys with the pieces of wood that are too small to fit our designs, so look very carefully while unpacking the box as there is surely a surprise in there for you!

While you unpack the box with your loved ones, remember to talk about the way these toys were mad.
We care for our planet, after all there is only one.
