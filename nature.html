We care for nature

We get our raw material from farms, wood producers and individuals like you. We receive all sorts of wood, so the process starts by selecting all the wood that still has potential in it. We make sure to find such potential in most of what we get.

We are proud to say 92% of what we receive is destined to building new toys, while the rest is used in making osb boxes and sawdust that we return to the farms so they can use in their animal’s habitats.

Local contribution

We work together with people that share our goals. We take most of our wood some donations, but we also buy old toys from the user. These are refurbished and passed on to the next family that is ready to give them some love.
