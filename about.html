Who are we?

We are a pair of nature enthusiasts. We feel sorry that so much prime material goes unused. It is for this reason we decided to create Små Poder.
Our goal is to transform the world of toys by upcycling old toys and reclaimed wood. We dream of helping families bond through building and using our products. We see it as a space that allows creativity and communion.

Our mission is to bring sustainability to your home. Reducing the use of water and other resources in the creation of new toys by recycling old toys, in this way, re-loving toys and helping parents, grandparents, aunts and uncles give presents with a meaning. Building these toys together with your loved ones is surely a memory that will be alive for ever.

Our vision is that in order to save the planet, we need to teach our children about where resources come from so they learn to love their planet.

We care about the future, and we know it starts in the present.
